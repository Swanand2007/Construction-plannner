About Smart Construction Planner

A small web tool that turns your plot, budget and lifestyle into a simple, architect‑friendly brief – before you spend on drawings.

What this planner does

You enter practical details such as plot size, facing direction, city band, budget and a few lifestyle flags. The planner converts them into a house type suggestion, approximate cost band and notes you can carry to a professional.

It does not replace a licensed architect or engineer. It simply helps owners come prepared to those discussions, with more realistic expectations and questions.

Who it is built for

  • First‑time plot owners planning their family home.
  • Students and young engineers learning about basic planning logic.
  • Small builders who want a quick conversation starter with clients.

How the logic works (short)

The current version uses simple rules and ranges:

  • Classifies 1BHK / 2BHK / 3BHK‑plus from usable built‑up area.
  • Uses city band to map to typical construction and interior rates.
  • Adds high‑level Vastu‑aware notes from plot facing.
  • Uses lifestyle and expansion flags to tweak room and circulation hints.

All numbers and messages are approximate and should be refined with local professionals before you build.
