About Smart Construction Planner

A small web tool that turns your plot, budget, floors and lifestyle into a simple, architect‑friendly brief – before you spend on drawings.

✨ What this planner does

You enter practical details such as plot size, facing direction, number of floors, city band, budget and lifestyle flags. The planner converts them into a house type suggestion (like "2BHK × 3 floors"), approximate cost band and notes you can carry to a professional.

🏠 New: Multi-floor planning

Choose 1-4 floors and see how costs, parking (stilt options), and house types automatically adjust. Perfect for G+1, G+2, or villa designs.

It does not replace a licensed architect or engineer. It simply helps owners come prepared to those discussions, with more realistic expectations and questions.

🎯 Who it is built for

  • First‑time plot owners planning their family home.
  • Students and young engineers learning construction logic.
  • Small builders wanting quick conversation starters.
  • Multi-floor planners optimizing plot usage.

⚙️ How the logic works

The current version uses smart rules and ranges:

  • BHK × Floors – Classifies layouts per floor (1BHK×3 = 3BHK total).
  • Region rates – Metro(₹2200/sqft) → Tier3(₹1500/sqft) construction.
  • Vastu hints from plot facing for entrance placement.
  • Lifestyle tweaks – Kids/elderly/work-from-home room suggestions.
  • Multi-floor costs – Construction × floors, architecture +20%.
